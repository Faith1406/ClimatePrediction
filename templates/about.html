About Us

NovAi is an innovative solution designed to combat the unpredictability of natural disasters by providing accurate, real-time predictions and actionable insights. Leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence and data analytics, NovAi addresses both primary calamities, such as heavy rainfall or earthquakes, and secondary impacts like landslides or infrastructural damage. By equipping individuals and authorities with timely information, our platform helps mitigate loss of life, reduce economic costs, and improve disaster preparedness on a national scale.

"Empowering communities to navigate natural disasters with confidence and clarity."

Built on advanced AI algorithms, including Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, NovAi processes and analyzes vast datasets to deliver reliable predictions. Utilizing powerful technologies like Intel Optimized PyTorch, OpenVINO, and Neural Compressor, the platform ensures seamless performance, even on resource-constrained systems. Beyond predictions, NovAi functions as a web-based public forum for updates and alerts, empowering users to make informed decisions during crises while fostering a collaborative approach to disaster response.

"Turning data into action, and action into safety."
